Frequently Asked Questions

How far can EVs travel?

Modern EVs typically have ranges of 250-500km on a single charge, which is sufficient for most daily driving needs in the Eastern Cape.

How long does charging take?

Home charging overnight takes 6-8 hours. Rapid charging at public stations can add 200km of range in 20-30 minutes.

Are EVs reliable in SA conditions?

EVs are well-suited to South African conditions and have fewer mechanical components that can fail compared to conventional vehicles.

What about load shedding?

Smart charging systems can work around load shedding schedules, and some EVs can even power your home during outages.

Are there government incentives?

Yes, various tax incentives and rebates are available for EV purchases, including reduced import duties and tax benefits.

What about resale value?

As EV adoption grows, resale values are becoming more stable, and the lower running costs make EVs attractive to second-hand buyers.
